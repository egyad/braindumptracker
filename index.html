Introduction

Brain Dump Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

  • All brain dump content (thoughts, notes, tasks) is stored locally on your device only
  • We do not transmit, collect, or store your brain dump content on any servers
  • Your data never leaves your device

Subscription Information

  • Subscription status and purchase history (managed by RevenueCat, Apple, and Google)
  • No personal information is collected beyond what is required for payment processing

Device Information

  • Minimal device identifiers required for subscription management only
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app functionality
  • To process and validate subscription purchases
  • To restore premium subscriptions across devices

Data Storage and Security

  • All brain dump data is stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage
  • We do not have access to your brain dump content
  • We cannot recover your data if you uninstall the app or lose your device
  • Subscription data is handled securely by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services solely for subscription management:

  • RevenueCat: For cross-platform subscription management
  • Apple App Store: For iOS in-app purchases
  • Google Play Store: For Android in-app purchases

These services operate under their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

  • Brain dump data: Remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Subscription data: Retained by payment processors as required for transaction records
  • We do not store any user data on our servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your subscription information through the app stores
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Request deletion of subscription records (contact payment processor)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
